    Catch them Young

    A conglomeration of sinking and elevated structures constructed with wood and rusty roofing sheets, Alaba - with a buldging population of over 3000 residents, is where deprivation meets chequered opportunities.The area is home to many low income earning families and the neighbourhood houses an army of young children whose future grows in uncertainty by the minute as a result of the highwave of crime in the vicinity. (source Punch NG)

    Catch them Young was born to give young children in this and similar environments the opportunity to show case their creativity and innovations along side the creme de la creme of the society. At CtY, we believe every child is special and has somthing instilled in him/her to make this world a better place. Emphasis on EVERY CHILD. CtY enables the less privileged in Alaba to acquire skills needed to be self reliant, employers of labour.

    At Catch them Young Club, a general knowledge on life and living is emphasized through sanitation and sanitization
